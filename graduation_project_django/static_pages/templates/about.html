OUR STORY

Whether pursuing an education, launching a career, or pivoting to a new one, it’s hard to do so alone. Dulleni is here to help and guide you every step of the way.

People spend half of their lives at work, and so for you to be happy, you have to be pursuing a career that is right for you; only then can you guarantee a happier and more fulfilled career path.

By that time, your personality, interests, and passion are established and well developed.


Dulleni’s mission is to help you explore your abilities and strengths, aligning them with available courses, programs, and later jobs.
